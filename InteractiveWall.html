Kinect Interactive Wall

The project can be used in reception room for customers.

Description

This is an outsourcing program for a hotel in Guangxi province, China, developed by my game studio. The development of this game was based on UE4 and Kinect. The game is for customers to kill time when they are waiting in the reception room of the hotel.

There will be one or a few matchstick men (up to five) in the game corresponding to the number of players captured by the Kinect placed in meeting room. The matchstick man will do actions according to what the player does, when the player shows lasso gesture by one hand or open gesture by two hands simultaneously, there will be different special effects. To add interestingness into the game, the matchstick man’s arms and legs are elastic. I was responsible for part of special effects design and implementation of multiplayer function.
