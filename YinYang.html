TerrySunty | Yin Yang Cross

Yin Yang Cross

I am responsible for the overall system design, overall level mechanism design and prototype function implementation of the game



Introduction

Inspiration:
Game Jam with the theme "Intersection".

Game Description：
This is a 3D platform puzzle game. The core gameplay of the game is built around the principle that Yin and Yang are compatible with the opposite attributes and repulsive with the same attributes. Players can interact with different Yin and Yang objects in the scene by switching their own Yin and Yang attributes to engage in certain puzzles and battles.

I. Theme and background settings

Theme Settings

Yin Yang Intersection

Background Settings：

The player is in a world where for unknown reasons, the yin and yang elements of the entire world are disordered, causing disharmony between yin and yang. As the world's elements regulator, players need to go to the location where the elements of yin and yang are disordered to bring yin and yang back into harmony.

II. Basic settings of the level

Mind map of the gameplay

Color Indication：

The game uses blue and red colors to represent objects with yin and yang attributes respectively. he objects with glowing materials are attack-able objects. Objects whose materials have a certain degree of transparency are objects that can be passed through. The gray brick material and the white glowing material indicate neutral (Yin and Yang harmony) objects.

Operation mechanism

  1. Movement：Common walking, running, jumping mechanism in 3D games.
  2. Switching Yin and Yang Attributes：When the number of times a player switches between yin and yang is greater than 0, the player can press the corresponding button to switch the yin and yang attributes that the character has. The yin and yang attributes provide visual cues to the player in the color of the material of the cat on top of the head.
  3. Magnet：The principle of opposites attract is utilized.The player can press the corresponding button to pull energy blocks that are different from the character's Yin Yang attributes in a certain range.If the attributes are the same, it cannot be attracted.The energy block will follow the player when the player is in the attracting (i.e. holding) state. When the player is in the attracting state, press the same button to put down the energy block.
  4. Launch：The principle of same poles repel each other is utilized.When players keep attracting(holding) state, once the players switch the Yin Yang attributes, the energy block that is attracted will be launched forward along the direction of the center of the camera's facing direction.
  5. Press：It utilizes the jumping mechanism of the player character and the mechanism of the buttons. When the player touches the collider of the button, the button performs a real-time press simulation.

Obstacles and props in the level

Level Mechanism Elements:
  1. Yin Spikes and Yang Spikes：
      a. Spikes will only do damage to characters whose Yin Yang attribute is same as Spikes' attributes.
      b. Characters with different Yin Yang attribute from Spikes' attributes can directly pass through spikes.
  2. Yin Yang Cannons
      a. The Cannons will be destroyed by the energy blocks with same Yin Yang attributes.被相同属性的能量块击毁。
      b. Fixed Attribute Cannons - Attributes will remain fixed.
      c. Attribute Switching Cannons - Attributes are switched at fixed time intervals within the level.
      d. Cannonball - Deals damage only to players with the same attribute, and the attributes of the cannonball are the same as the current attributes of the cannon that fired it out.
  3. Platforms: For players with the same attributes, the player will be launched upwards, for players with different attributes, the platform will cancel the collision and the player will fall directly through the platform.
  4. Portals: Serve as a mechanism to get to the next level. The portal will appear at the end of the level after a successful puzzle solving challenge.

Puzzle Mechanism:
  1. Bagua trigrams:
      a. Presented in a nine-box grid.
      b. Players need to follow the trigrams by firing energy blocks to activate the nine grids into patterns corresponding to the trigrams (Li/Kan trigrams).
      c. After hitting the wrong square, the progress of the trigram puzzle is reset.
      d. It can be activated only after making contact with the energy block corresponding to the yin and yang attributes. For example, the nine grid of the Kan trigram puzzle should make contact with the energy block of the Yin attribute.
      e. Spawn the portal after successful puzzle solving.
  2. Button Array
      a. Players follow the prompts and step on the buttons in order.
      b. Reset: Progress is reset when the player does not step on the buttons in order.
      c. Activation: After stepping on all of them in order, a portal will be spawned.

Level Rules:

Level 1 (Yin attribute dominant)
  1. Players need to learn to use the ability to switch between yin and yang and go through the spikes to press the buttons of the button array in order to spawn a portal to the next level, according to the order of the arrangement of the platforms marked with numbers in the level 1 layout.
  2. Touching a spike with the same attribute will kill the player.
  3. Touching the same attribute of the platform players will be launched, touching the different attribute of the platform players directly fall through the platform.
  4. After falling off the platform, the player will die after touching the damage volume at the bottom.
  5. Reborn at the last touched checkpoint after death.
Level 2 (Yang attribute dominated)
  1. Players incorporate the ability to switch between yin and yang learned in the first level, and learn to use the ability to attract and launch energy blocks in this level.
  2. The player enters the corresponding trigger area/volume of the cannon, the cannon is activated, and the cannon fires at the player in a tracking way. The cannon fires once every 1.5 seconds.
  3. Players follow the trigram hints, use the Yang attribute energy blocks, hit the Li-fire trigram to spawn a portal to the next level.
  4. Touching the same attribute of the platform players will be launched, touching the different attribute of the platform players directly fall through the platform.
  5. After falling off the platform, the player will die after touching the damage volume at the bottom.
  6. Reborn at the last touched checkpoint after death.
Level 3 (Yin and Yang)
  1. Players incorporate the abilities learned in the previous two levels and follow the trigram hints to solve the puzzle twice in order to spawn the portal for victory.
  2. Added attribute switching cannon to enhance the level difficulty, and the cannon will switch attributes every 3 seconds.
  3. The player enters the corresponding trigger area/volume of the cannon, the cannon is activated, and the cannon fires at the player in a tracking way. The cannon fires once every 1.5 seconds.
  4. Touching the same attribute of the platform players will be launched, touching the different attribute of the platform players directly fall through the platform.
  5. After falling off the platform, the player will die after touching the damage volume at the bottom.
  6. Reborn at the last touched checkpoint after death.
  7. At the last puzzle, once the button is pressed, the puzzle will do the check. If both trigrams are correct, then the portal will be spawned, otherwise, the process of the puzzle solving will be reset.

III. Design Process

The level mechanism and placement are mainly constructed around the idea of yin and yang reconciliation and same attributes repulsion.
Because water and fire are common elements for most players, it is relatively easy to accept and understand, so the two yin and yang trigrams of water and fire were selected as the first two levels of the elements.


Yang: Li - Fire；Yin: Kan - Water

Players' interestingness curve in three levels is expected as the following. It fluctuates but gradually rises overall.

Interestingness


Level 1
Level 1 Flowchart
Level Design Purpose

To let players learn to use the ability to switch between yin and yang, while having basic knowledge of the level mechanics of yin and yang being compatible and same nature being repulsive. And it provides a lower threshold for players to get started and enter the level flow more easily.

    Method:
  1. To give players a lower threshold, in this level, only a simple platform jumping mechanism and yin-yang switching mechanism are introduced. The puzzle just requires players to simply remember the order of their jumping platforms.
  2. In order to provide players with an initial understanding of the yin/yang compatible/repulsive mechanism,in the level 1 layout, the red yang platform is placed behind the neutral platform near the yin platform marked with the number 5.The player is in the yin state and will fall directly from the red platform as they walk straight towards it. Players who have just seen the "Yin-Yang is compatible" hint on the platform may feel confused and try to relate the hint to the falling experience. This is the initial stage for players to understand the compatibility of yin and yang.
  3. In order to motivate players to switch between yin and yang states and to further understand the mechanism of yin and yang compatibility/repulsion, spikes with yin and yang attributes were designed and introduced into the first level. After the player falls from the red platform, the player can directly see the yin and yang switch ball and text prompts in front of the player. If the player ignores the hints and switch props and walks straight to the spikes, the character will die directly. In order to pass the spikes, the player will have the motivation to use the yin and yang switching mechanism. After using the Yin-Yang switching mechanism to pass the spikes with opposite attributes, the player will have a better understanding of the mechanism of Yin-Yang compatibility and repulsion. Thus, the player will learn the ability to switch between yin and yang.
  4. The first level of the puzzle challenge is mainly used to adjust the player's interest curve and is relatively easy. At the high platform, there is text that prompts players to turn back and pay attention to their jump path. The text hints at the puzzle make the player associate the puzzle with the previous path hints to solve the puzzle. The simpler puzzle challenges also make it easy for players to gain a sense of accomplishment and enter a positive feedback loop in the early stages.Thus, players are motivated to explore the following game content.
  5. To give the player a clear visual indication, a cut scene of the portal is played after the puzzle is successfully solved.


Level 2
Level 2 Flowchart
Level Design Purpose:

Enrich the mechanics content and introduce battle-oriented level mechanics to increase the difficulty of the game and bring players a sense of surprise, challenge, and achievement. The mechanics of attracting and firing energy blocks are introduced and made available to players to learn in the process of level 2.

    Method:
  1. To enrich the content of the mechanism, and the introduction of combat-oriented level mechanism.A reference to the mechanism of attracting and launching objects in the game Control was made.To conform to the overall setting of yin and yang compatibility and repulsion,The energy block is set to be an object with yin and yang attributes as well, and can only be attracted when it is different from the player's attributes, and launched outward when it is the same as the player's attributes (the launching is a reference to the same-pole repulsion of magnets).
  2. In order to gradually increase the difficulty and avoid giving players frustration too early.The starting cannon trigger area is divided into three separate areas, each time the player enters an area only one cannon trigger, less difficult to dodge and less likely to die. After the player learns the mechanism of destroying cannons , the trigger area becomes a whole. After the player enters the five cannons trigger area, five cannons will shoot at the player together (there is an interval time for firing, giving the player room to operate). In this way, the step-up in difficulty is completed.
  3. In order to conform to the setting of yin and yang compatibility and same repulsion, and to give players a deeper understanding of the mechanics surrounding this setting, the cannonball from the cannon also has yin and yang attributes.No damage to the player when the attributes are different, and damage to the player when the attributes are the same.
  4. In order to allow players to understand the mechanism of same poles repulsion of energy blocks through trial and error,at the prompt to destroy the cannon, two types of energy blocks are placed, and players can understand the same poles repulsion mechanism of energy blocks by attracting different energy blocks and firing them at the cannon.
  5. To give players a sense of surprise and to make better use of the attraction and launch mechanism.Here the final puzzle design is associated with the Bagua trigrams of yin and yang trigrams by setting up a nine-cubic grid composed of nine neutral cubes. And trigrams and text hints are placed nearby for players to reference. When the player get correct the trigram by hitting cubes with energy blocks, the puzzle is successfully solved. Players After discovering that energy blocks can also be used to solve puzzles, players will gain a certain sense of surprise and learn the trigram puzzle solving mechanism.
  6. To prompt the player, the trigram square can be hit, set the material of the cube to be the same glowing material as the cannon and energy block.


Level 3
Level 3 Flowchart
Level Design Purpose:

Referring to Nintendo's idea of designing the levels to be "起承转合", the third level mainly plays the role of "twist" and "thrown away". It is a challenge to test the player's mastery of the first two levels. In order to keep the player in a "flow", the introduction of new mechanisms with minor modifications, to a certain extent, to enhance the difficulty of the game.

    Method:
  1. In order to give players a small increase in the difficulty of the game on the basis of the previous one, keeping in the " flow " channel,the Attributes Switching Cannon is introduced。Players have already understood and mastered the mechanism of the cannon and the method of defeating the cannon in the second level, so in the third level the method of switching the attributes of the cannon has slightly increased the difficulty of the combat based on the existing one, players need to do some thinking, but can master the new mechanism easily.
  2. In order to give players a better sense of challenge, surprise and achievement, This level refers to the concept of "twist", the first two levels of the machine obstacles are placed in the level, and more closely, to improve the difficulty of a certain degree.同时为了符合“阴阳交错”的主题,At the same time, in order to comply with the theme of " Intersection of Yin and Yang", this level of different yin and yang attributes of the mechanism / obstacles are placed more frequently, and the ambient lighting also made the corresponding staggered arrangement.
  3. To pave the way for the final puzzle and give the player hints,a yin attribute trigram puzzle is set up in the middle of the level. This also examines the player's ability to observe and think，In the distance there is a kan - water trigram hint, if the player can observe, with a previous level of the kan trigram experience, can know that the current need to play the kan trigram.
  4. In order to comply with the end of the harmony of yin and yang again, the final puzzle refers to the concept of "Li" representing the south and "Kan" representing the north in the Bagua, and sets the mechanism of pressing the button to move the two trigrams to the corresponding direction.The victory point portal in the third level also references this concept, with particle effects of water and fire placed above and below the yin and yang diagrams, respectively.
  5. The textual hints within the level refer to some extent to the content of ancient texts related to Bagua.

Mechanisms that were considered but not implemented:
  1. Attracting range effects:When the player presses the attract button, a semi-circular magnetic effect is generated centered on the player's character to indicate the player's available attracting range.
  2. Timed flip-flop platform: The platform has two sides, one yin and one yang, and is flipped every fixed time. Gives damage to the player when it is consistent with the player's character attributes. Used to increase the difficulty of the level in an attempt to solidify the player in the flow experience.
  3. Energy Block Chain:The yin and yang energy blocks, in accordance with certain paths, are staggered to conduct energy, and if not for the yin-yang-yin-yang placement, energy will not be conducted.There are energy emitters (with yin and yang attributes) at the beginning of the path and energy receivers at the end. When the energy receiver receives energy, it opens the corresponding door or generates a platform for the player to pass through. This mechanism is used to enrich the player's level experience.
  4. When the player is in a state of attraction, the camera is shifted to facilitate the player to aim

Possible subsequent design directions:

Introduce other trigrams in the Bagua, and integrate heaven, earth, water, fire, thunder, wind, mountain, and lake for the corresponding mechanism design. To enrich the player experience and enhance the depth of the level strategy, in order to bring players a sense of surprise, challenge and achievement.
