Yin Huo 烎火

Got third prize in Chengdu district
(there are four competition district in China: Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Chengdu)

Project Description

This game named Yin Huo (烎火) was developed with UE4 in the Perfect World’s Game Jam, 2018. The topic of the game jam was “烎 (Yin)”, an Chinese character, which means the brightness originally in the dictionary, and it is popular in the network and is usually used to describe a player’s fighting spirit, since the structure of this character is similar to “开火( open fire in English)” from top to bottom.

According to the topic’s meaning, we associated a group of fireflies who have fighting spirit with the topic. Our game is a kind of puzzle game, it tells a story about how fireflies take a magic bead (the white luminous ball) back to save their home. Players need to control and scale the firefly group to interact with environmental elements, like flowers and leaves, to move the magic bead to the destination. In the game, the firefly group’s formation can be sparse or tight, and the player can shoot part of fireflies to the flower, leaves or other interactive elements, then the environmental element will change its own status for fireflies to move the magic bead. We encourage players to explore the interactive world in the game in this way. This game finally got third prize in the game jam competition.

I was responsible for story background of the game, interactive NPC’s design and part of levels’ design.
