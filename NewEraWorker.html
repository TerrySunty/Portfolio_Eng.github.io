New Era Worker

2021 Global Game Jam Project（Not finished）
（Responsible for development, level design, and part of narrative design）

Demo Video




This is a 2D platformer game based on a ironic story. The narration includes satire on endless work due to capitalism，and delivers a sentence: behind the seemingly lost things in life there may be unexpected joys, the so-called misfortunes and blessings depend on.

The protagonist's monologue: "I am 2049, a working robot in a new era. All my parts are as prescribed by others. The daily plan is to work non-stop... But one day, I don't want to go on like this anymore, so I ran and ran... I lost a piece of iron that I've been carrying for a long time, I don't know what it was, and even lost my leg to run faster, but I found..."


2021 Global Game Jam Theme

According to the theme Lost and Found, Me and my team think we should take "gain in loss" as a base to build the game.

Inspired by Gris , I decided that let the protagonist gain a new ability like Gris, after every lost in the adventure, until the protagonist find desired things.

When developing the game, the Internet was at the hot spot of "打工人". So, I thought about whether "打工人" can be combined with "gains and losses". In the end, it was decided to set the protagonist of the game as the robot No.2049 who wants to escape the endless work in the factory of the future. In the form of the robot losing parts on the road of adventure but accidentally gaining new abilities, players can gain a "gain and loss" gaming experience. For example, Robot No.2049 tripped over a large rock and broke the leg assembly, and the spring in the body come out, and the robot also gains the ability to jump.
