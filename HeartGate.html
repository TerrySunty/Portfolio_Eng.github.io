Demo Video



2020 CiGA Game Jam Theme

Based on the elements in the painting - the huge crack, the courier box filled with the cracks in the floor, the ants, the cage in the innermost room, the girl looking into the crack, my team members and I conceived the following game story backgrounds:

  • 1. Criticize the pre-consumerism that exists in today's society - the room symbolizes the girl's heart, and the crack symbolizes the girl's despair of life. Because girls spend uncontrollably ahead of time to satisfy their material desires, it brings huge difficulties to the girls' lives, and at the same time, it also puts a huge pressure on the girls' hearts and makes them feel hopeless about life.

  • The girl has a mental illness and protects herself by locking herself in a house and cutting off from the outside world. Live a life through takeout and online shopping. But in this way, the estrangement between her and the outside world is growing.

  • 3. The girl lacks love since she was a child. When she grows up, she can only fill her inner world with material things. Her inner world is bleak and she yearns for a positive life.

    • Finally, the game was designed with second story background.
